## 🎭 **Animation Details:**

### **Timing Functions:**
- **Ease:** Standard transitions
- **Cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1):** Material Design easing
- **Cubic-bezier(0.68, -0.55, 0.265, 1.55):** Bounce effect

### **Durations:**
- **Fast:** 0.3s (hover states)
- **Medium:** 0.4-0.6s (cards, buttons)
- **Slow:** 0.8-1s (entrance animations)
- **Infinite:** Continuous animations (pulse, float)
